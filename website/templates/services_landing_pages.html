Common Landing Page Mistakes

Avoid these costly landing page mistakes

Research shows that poor landing page design kills conversions before visitors even reach your offer. Without optimization, businesses lose 50-70% of potential customers to preventable mistakes.

The biggest error is generic messaging that does not match ad copy. When visitors click an ad promising one thing but land on a page saying something different, up to 60% leave immediately. Similarly, weak call-to-action can reduce conversions by 35%.

Professional landing page design eliminates these risks through conversion-focused copywriting, strategic layout, and continuous testing. Expert designers know how to craft compelling headlines, place trust signals effectively, and optimize forms to maximize submissions.
