What is AEO?

Understanding Answer Engine
Optimization

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing your content so online response tools can cite and feature your brand in answers generated by AI-powered engines.

Unlike traditional SEO that targets only search ranking, AEO focuses on becoming the source AI systems trust and reference when answering user queries.

AI-generated search is scaling fast. Without AEO, your competitors with optimized content can own the conversation while your brand remains invisible.
